Through my extracurricular activities I am constantly growing my knowledge and meeting wonderful people along the way. I would love to get to know you as well! Feel free to contact me using resources on my contacts page. You can also find more information about my projects and interests on the porfolio page

One of my skills include writing. I am an Alumini of the University of Iowa's Writers Room program. Here I worked with Proffesors and talented creative writers. Through this 9 week program I made many great friendships and developed my writing skills. I am also publishing a reasearch paper in 2021 Fall on the Water and Sanitation crisis in Zimbabwe. During my free time I love to journal and write spoken word poetry.

Another one of my skills include programming. I am passionate about improving my coding skills, so I started a Girls Who Code club at my school. I work with various students and we all learn to program together using resources from Scratch, Trinket, GWC, etc. I also work on my programming skills through FIRST Technology Challenge and use Block code and am now transitioning to JavaScirpt.

Public speaking is one my favorite activities. I absolutely enjoy the rush of emotions and having the ability to voice my prespective and thoughts. Through my schools Speech and Debate team I compete in events such as Original Oratory, Poetry, Prose, Public Forum,and Lincoln Douglas.
